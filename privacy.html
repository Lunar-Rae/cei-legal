Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the internal CEI Coatings accounting integration ("the Application") handles data. The Application is operated by CEI Coatings for its own internal business use and is not offered to the public.

1. Scope

The Application connects to CEI Coatings' own QuickBooks Online company file via Intuit's official API, using credentials authorized by CEI Coatings. It is used solely by CEI Coatings staff and authorized operators.

2. Information We Access

The Application accesses accounting information from QuickBooks Online, including customer names, invoices, and accounts-receivable balances, strictly to support internal accounts-receivable operations (for example, identifying past-due invoices and preparing reminder drafts for internal review).

3. How Information Is Used

Accessed data is used only for CEI Coatings' internal accounting and operations. It is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties, and is not used for advertising or any purpose beyond internal business operations.

4. Storage and Security

Data is processed on CEI Coatings' own systems. Access credentials are stored securely and restricted to authorized operators. Access tokens may be cached locally to maintain the connection and are not disclosed to third parties.

5. Data Retention

Information is retained only as long as needed for internal accounting operations and is removed when no longer required.

6. Third-Party Services

The Application relies on Intuit QuickBooks Online. Use of QuickBooks data is governed by Intuit's own terms and privacy practices.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy: cei@ceicoatings.com.
